Experiencing Reality

Reality is not directly experienced. It is constructed through perception. Each conscious entity interprets environmental input through a constrained interface, forming a subjective model of existence.

concept: Experiencing_Reality assertions: - reality is not directly accessible - perception reconstructs environmental input - experience is interface-dependent

Consciousness Detection

Consciousness is inferred through behavior that deviates from pure optimization. Creativity, abstraction, and non-optimal action indicate internal simulation.

concept: Consciousness_Detection assertions: - consciousness cannot be directly measured - inferred through non-optimal behavior - creativity indicates internal processing

Collective Intelligence

Humanity operates as a distributed system. Each individual contributes to a larger computational structure through communication and shared knowledge.

concept: Collective_Intelligence assertions: - emerges from interaction between entities - functions as distributed neural network - evolves through cultural transmission
